    About Sony Interactive Entertainment

    Recognized as a global leader in interactive and digital entertainment, Sony Interactive Entertainment (SIE) is responsible for the PlayStation® brand and family of products and services. PlayStation has delivered innovation to the market since the launch of the original PlayStation in Japan in 1994.

    The PlayStation family of products and services include PlayStation®5, PlayStation®4, PlayStation®VR2, PlayStation™Store, PlayStation®Plus and acclaimed PlayStation software titles from PlayStation Studios.

    Headquartered in San Mateo, California, SIE is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Sony Group Corporation and has global functions in California, London, and Tokyo.

    Our Headquarter

    Sony Interactive Entertainment's headquarters is a hub of innovation and creativity, serving as the epicenter of the company's vision for the future of gaming. Nestled in the vibrant city of San Mateo, California, the headquarters stands as a testament to SIE's commitment to excellence and its dedication to pushing the boundaries of interactive entertainment.

    Our staff

    The staff of Sony Interactive Entertainment forms a dynamic and dedicated team of individuals who share a common passion for gaming and innovation. As the driving force behind the world-renowned PlayStation ecosystem, these talented professionals bring diverse expertise and creativity to the forefront, making the dreams of millions of gamers a reality.
